This file takes a list of people in 'Data/input.csv' and makes randomized superbowl squares in .csv format outputed to 'Data/output.csv'

How 'Data/input.csv' is layed out
"Name, Money \n"

Name is the person's name.
Money is the amount of money recieved from that person 

To change the number of money per people edit line 6 in 'Person.java'
The output is in .csv format and is seperated into 4 10 x 10 grids verticly, these are the tables to publish and format them make to make them look nice
Rules $75 for 1st and 3rd quarter, $100 for half, $200 for final score $600 max total
total profit $150
